Spaghetti Bolognese

Description:

Spaghetti-Bolognese is a easy to cook dish for 100 persons. It's very delicious.

Ingredients:

  • 500g beef
  • 50g butter
  • 400g tomatoes
  • 1 Pr salt
  • 1 Pr pepper
  • 300g parmigana cheese (good one!)
  • 500g spaghetti

Steps:

  1. heat the meat with a pan till it is brown and a little bit burned
  2. put in the tomatoes and cook it for about 5 hours
  3. cook the spaghetties for 8 min
  4. serve spaghetties with bolognese and cheese
