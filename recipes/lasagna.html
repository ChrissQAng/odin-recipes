Lasagna

Description:

Lasagna is a delicous meal made of meat, bechamell and nudle-dough.

Ingredients:

  • 500g beef
  • 200g butter
  • 100g flour
  • 1 Pr salt
  • 1 Pr pepper
  • 300g cheese (good one!)
  • lasagna-plates

Steps:

  1. heat the meat with a pan till it is brown and a little bit burned
  2. for bechamell heat the butter and stir in the flour; mix it for a very long time
  3. take a casserole dish and put in alternately meat, bechamell and lasagna-plates
  4. push it in the oven for 5 hours; 200 Celtius
